The Journey

Join us for an immersive six-hour paddling expedition through the glacial waters of the Squamish estuary. This isn't just a workout; it's a silent meditation. We'll navigate through narrow channels of reeds, watching for bald eagles and harbor seals that frequent the area during the salmon run.

Equipment is provided, but we recommend bringing thermal base layers and a dry change of clothes. We'll stop halfway at a secluded pebble beach for a forest-brewed coffee and light snacks provided by Marcus.
